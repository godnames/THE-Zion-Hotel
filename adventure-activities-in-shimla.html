Camping

Duration 3-4 Hours (Day Activity)
Start Time: 10:30

A drive from Hotel Zion to the camping area far from the hustle and bustle of busy city lives, Shimla boasts of serene landscapes with thick forests, cedar timberlands and the fresh mountain air that possesses a magnetic attraction to all nature lovers who love to visit this captivating area. The peaceful magnificence of the mild breeze takes away all the stress of everyday life.
Needless to say, camping tours in Shimla are the best way to experience a breathtaking holiday. The most popular camping sites are at high altitudes to experience the sheer gorgeousness of the snow-clad peaks and valleys. The camping excursion is combined with Food, Lodging, adventure activities, Bonfires, Burma Bridge, Bamboo Bridge, Tyre Swing, Mogli walk and other activities.
Delicious Himachali Food such as – Rice, Chapati, Salad, Papad, Dal, Mix veg, Paneer dish and dessert.

Rafting

Duration Full day. Starts at 10:30 AM
River rafting is one of the most awe-inspiring adventure sports in that can be done throughout the year. However, the best time for river rafting in all its glory is March to June and October to March. The rivers are fresh and rapid, flowing with unobstructed connectivity.
Packages for rafting – Short River Rafting that goes up to 7km to 9km.
Places, where you can experience river rafting and white water rafting are:
- River Sutlej
- Tattapani

Trekking

Duration: - 4-5 Hours (Day Activity) Starts at 10:30
One of the robust adventures is trekking. On to the snowy mountains of the Himalayas or interspersed rocky terrains, treks into Pine trees are a thrilling getaway for those seeking solidarity and seclusion.
Places where you can have a venturesome trekking experience are:
Churdhar Trek (3647 m)
Karol Tibba (2240 m)
Shimla-Jalori Pass Trek (3120 m)
Hatu Peak (3400 m)
Shali Tibba (2867 m)
Other Easy Treks: Kamna Devi, Dhanu Devta Temples, Chadwick Waterfall.

Bonfire

Starts at 07:30 PM till 10:30 PM
A drive of 05 Km from The hotel into the pine tree forest where bonfire starts organized with Himachali food and music.

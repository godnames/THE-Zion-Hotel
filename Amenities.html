Quality Hotel & Suites Services Providing by Professionals

Our rooms in Shimla are equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, television, and private bathrooms. Some rooms also have balconies with mountain views. It is one of the best places to stay in Shimla, located in a prime location, and is easily accessible from all major tourist spots.

Ananta Multi-Cuisine Restaurant

Ananta is home to much-celebrated multi-cuisine and fine-dining speciality restaurant in Shimla. Connoisseurs enjoy the authentic Himachali, Chinese, new-age Indian and other cuisines on offer in stunning settings. Reserve a table now to enjoy a blissful culinary experience! In our bid to deliver authentic, undiluted experiences, we’ve introduced a unique regional home-style dining experience. Always prepared with a local touch, the home-style cooking ensures that you maintain good health throughout your stay. And at the same time, it gives you the opportunity to feel your food in the local delicacies.

Ananta Bar

Enjoy a drink in our spacious lounge bar, is a truly enjoyable experience – the views of the mountains, with the summits of hills will help to ensure that you will have a “relaxing drink”. There is a full range of bar drinks including a wide selection of spirits, Whisky, Brandy and Liquors, soft drinks and beer including. The wine list includes a variety and styles of wine with bottle and glass. A selection of teas and coffees are available throughout the day in the Bar.

Kids Zone & Play Area

The Hotel offers a lot to keep your kids relished. We have a dedicated kids' club, an activity centre with indoor games, a Nintendo Wii station for video-game lovers, plus a kids pool with day beds. They even have Play Stations and DVDs in the rooms! And all of them get a special kids menu from which they can choose themselves. If you and kids are both board game lovers, head out for a game of Ludo, scrabble or chess and bond with your children over some food and games. If you're travelling with teens, they've got that covered too with a special gaming arcade. With numerous indoor games organised specially for children, it is no wonder that this property is a family favourite.

Event Spaces

Whether your wedding is going to be an intimate gathering or a bustling ballroom celebration, Zion Shimla offers unique arrangements to host events such as the ceremony, reception, rehearsal dinner, and more, as well as pre-event functions including the bridal shower or bachelor and bachelorette party.

The Gym

In an effort to offer guests a stress-free and Healthy stay, The ZION has outfitted its fitness center with top-of-the-line cardio and strength-training equipment. The result is to provide an incredible range of motion, treadmills that gently adjust in speed and incline, and climbers that challenge even the most dedicated athletes. The machines also include interactive technology that can provide custom training routines and instructions on how to use the equipment. Plus, the fitness center’s floor-to-ceiling windows and clean atmosphere make it a pleasant space to work up a sweat, day or night.

Travel Desk

Valet Parking
